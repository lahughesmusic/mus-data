Let's Get Some Music!!

Feel free to preview and print whatever strikes your interest. I ask that you not share this site with others. It is intended for students taking piano lessons from Lea Hughes only. If a piece does strike an interest and it seems too difficult, please print it anyway and we can work on simplifying it at the next lesson. There is usually a musical work around to make the piece fit the student's skill level if the desire is there to learn it.
